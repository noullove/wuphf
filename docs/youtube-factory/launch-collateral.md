Back Office AI shows founder-led teams how to automate the boring but expensive parts of running a business: inbox triage, follow-ups, reporting, onboarding, support, SOPs, and meeting cleanup.

Every video is built around one real workflow, one clear outcome, and one operating asset you can actually use. No hype. No fake passive-income nonsense. Just cleaner systems for small teams that need leverage.
